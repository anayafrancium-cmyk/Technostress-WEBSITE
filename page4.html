Effects on Health & Work

Effects

Physical Health Issues: Chronic fatigue, headaches, eye strain (visual fatigue), back/neck pain, and sleep disorders.

Mental and Emotional Strain: Anxiety, burnout, depression, irritability, and feelings of inadequacy or being overwhelmed by information.

Work Performance Deterioration: Decreased productivity, loss of concentration, decreased motivation, and increased absenteeism.

Behavioral and Social Changes: Resistance to organizational change, increased employee turnover (intention to quit), and social isolation, or conflict due to constant connectivity.
