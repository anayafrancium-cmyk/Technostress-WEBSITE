Digital Well-being Tips

To reduce techno-stress, consider these habits:

  1. Set "No-Tech" zones in your home (like the dining table).
  2. Turn off non-essential notifications.
  3. Practice the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
  4. Create Tech-Free Zones/Times: Designate areas like the dining room or bedroom as gadget-free, and avoid screens during meals or 30-60 minutes before bed to improve sleep
  5. Manage Notifications: Turn off non-essential notifications to minimize distractions and check messages on your own schedule.
